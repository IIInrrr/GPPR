About Ageism

Ageism is a form of discrimination that negatively impacts older individuals, especially in the job market. This site aims to raise awareness and provide support for job seekers aged 50 and above in Kazakhstan.

Proposed Solutions

We propose the following solutions to aid older job seekers:

  1. Online job portals tailored for older workers.
  2. Workshops on digital skills to enhance employability.
  3. Networking events to connect older job seekers with potential employers.

Mental Health Support

Unemployment can affect mental well-being. If you are feeling overwhelmed, you are not alone.

  • Visit MentalHealth.kz for free counseling
  • WHO Mental Health Support
  • Talk to someone — even a short conversation can help lighten the burden.
